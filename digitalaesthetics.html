New Workshop

Empowering students to speak, lead & succeed.

A premium hands-on workshop teaching design fundamentals, branding basics, and social-media visual creation — all distilled into a powerful 3-hour experience.

Enroll Now View Course Breakdown
Overview

Logic Meets Imagination

Digital aesthetics isn't just about making things look "pretty"—it's about intentional design, branding coherence, and visual storytelling. This workshop bridges the gap between raw creativity and professional execution.

Perfect for beginners & creators
Practical, tool-based learning
Immediate portfolio outcome

What You'll Learn

Design Foundations

Master typography, spacing, hierarchy, and grid systems. Learn why some layouts work and others fail.

Brand Identity

Understand logo usage, color psychology, and how to create a consistent visual language.

Social Media Visuals

Create high-impact carousels, stories, and reels covers that stop the scroll.

Tools & Workflow

Get hands-on with tools like Figma or Canva. Learn efficient workflows for batch creation.

Visual Storytelling

Align your visuals with your message. Learn to guide the viewer's eye through composition.

Practical Projects

Don't just listen—build. You'll create a moodboard and a polished social post during the session.

3-Hour Session Breakdown

0:00 – 0:10

Introduction & Warm-up

Overview of the session goals, quick introductions, and analyzing good vs. bad design examples.

0:10 – 0:45

Design Foundations

Deep dive into typography, color theory, grid layouts, and visual hierarchy.

0:45 – 1:20

Branding Essentials

Creating a cohesive identity system. Mini case study on visual language.

1:20 – 1:30

Break

Short recharge.

1:30 – 2:10

Social Media & Storytelling

Translating brand into content. Storytelling exercise for engagement.

2:10 – 2:40

Tools & Creation

Live demo of Figma/Canva workflows. Creating a real post from scratch.

2:40 – 3:00

Review & Wrap-up

Q&A, resource pack distribution, and final feedback.

Career Edge

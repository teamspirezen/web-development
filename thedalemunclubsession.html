Course Syllabus

Ice Breakers: build confidence, introductions, basics of diplomacy and interaction.

Mastering the basics and Rules of Procedure (RoP) — agendas and framing stances.

Advanced maneuvers: breaking topics into sub-issues and solution design.

Crafting excellence: formal formats, drafting resolutions and presentation technique.

Research Wizards: thorough research methods and reliable sourcing.

Fragmentation and collection: organizing research into topics and stances.

Skill building: solo performer — introductions, interview-style practice, first impressions.

Skill building: team talk — addressing crowds, leading a team and presenting ideas.

Interactive simulation & Q&A — practice sessions with live feedback.

Wrapping up — review, reflection and next steps: you talk, we listen.
